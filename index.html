Who We Are

Driven by Purpose, Powered by Tech.

TechNova Infinium Pvt. Ltd. was founded on September 19, 2015, in Bangalore with a singular mission: to empower businesses through innovative, scalable, and future-ready IT solutions.

What began as a boutique consulting firm has evolved into a mid-sized digital powerhouse. The name "TechNova" represents new-age technology, while "Infinium" stands for infinite possibilities. Together, they define our commitment to limitless growth.

We don't just act as vendors; we are strategic partners who bridge the gap between complex business problems and elegant technical solutions.
